"$size":- Used as aggregation operator to extract length of an array in a stage of a agg pipeline.
eg:
db.movies.aggregate([{"$project":{"array_size":["$size" : "$array_field_name"]}}])
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
"$split":- to split a string value based on a delimiter.
db.movies.aggregate([{"$project":{"$split":["$field_name", "delimiter"]}}])
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
